Our Co-op
Program 
							ThoughtsWin Systems proudly collaborates with institutions such as the University of BC’s Faculty of Science, School of Engineering and Sauder School of Business in Canada, Shri Keshav Institute of Technology (SKIT) and Poornima College in Jaipur, India in an innovative co-op program, blending academic insights with practical industry experience. This initiative offers students real-world exposure, while simultaneously contributing fresh perspectives and ideas to our dynamic team.
Our appreciation goes out to the Co-op and Intern teams at these institutions for their pivotal roles in this partnership, fostering a bridge between education and practical application. Through this program, we’re not just sharing knowledge; we’re cultivating future leaders and exploring new frontiers in business innovation.
Our joint ventures are a testament to our commitment to nurturing talent and supporting the next generation of professionals. We look forward to the continued success and mutual growth this collaboration brings.
